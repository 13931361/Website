Lightning Wallets

with zero configuration

On Bluewallet you can use custodial Lightning wallets (or choose to run your own node), that allows you to seemless use the Lightning Network for payments, without the need to open channels, be online, provide liquidity or operate and mantain a node. Our goal is to remove friction and provide a great user experience. This trade off comes with pros and cons, please read more about them on the LNDhub announcement article.

Features

Refillavailable

Allows you top up or refill your Lightning wallet with Bitcoin. It requires an on-chain Bitcoin wallet to use it. It requires 3 confirmations to be validated.

Withdrawcoming soon

Allows you get your funds back to a Bitcoin on-chain wallet. At the moment Bluewallet redirects you to zigzag.io while the feature is not ready.

Sendavailable

Make payments or send Satoshis after scanning a QR code or having an address. Allows to Scan QR codes, Use screenshots of QR codes or a Lightning addresses. Requires having funds previously.

Receiveavailable

Recieve payments by generating an invoice. Generates a QR code and an address with expiration time that can be easily shared. Allows to open a direct channel with the node.

Backup/export Keyavailable

Through the wallet options menu you will find the option to backup/export your key. This is a LNDhub key that will only work on services like bluewallet that use LNDhub.
