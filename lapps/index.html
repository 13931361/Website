marketplace

The Marketplace brings to Bitcoin and Lightning the first Lapp Browser.

Enabling Bluewallet users to easily discover where to use and which services support the Lightning Network.

Lightning Apps

Lightning Apps or “Lapps” are services powered or with payment support for the Lightning Network.

They should use WebLN as their standard protocol to interact with the web. Please read more about WebLN.

Clever pay and withdraw

Bluewallet, through our new Lapp Browser, will try to automatically identify the payment or the withdraw feature when these are supported by the service or lapp.

This makes the experience way smoother by reducing the necessary steps to complete the payment process and by not having to change between apps.
