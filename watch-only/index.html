Watch only

Watch only wallets allows you to keep an eye on your cold storage or paper wallet without touching your private key. Easily import your public address, xpub or ypub and watch it from your app without ever touching it.

Features

xpub bip44

a xpub address derived from bip44 refers to the non segwit addresses. These addresses begin with a 1

ypubbip49

a ypub address derived from bip49 refers to the segwit compatible addresses. These addresses begin with a 3

zpubbip84

a zpub address derived from bip84 refers to the native segwit addresses. These addresses begin with a bc1

Public Address

You can also monitor any public address that you may have. And see what is happening with this address

Receive

All watch-only type wallets also allows you to receive and generate QR codes for that purpose

How does it work?

1

Tap on add wallet icon

2

Go to the option import wallet

3

Import with text or by QR code
